Introduction

DeFiners protocol is a peer-to-peer* system designed for exchanging cryptocurrencies (ERC-20 Tokens) on the Ethereum blockchain. The protocol is implemented as a set of persistent, non-upgradable smart contracts; designed to prioritize censorship resistence, security, self-custody, and to function without any trusted intermediaries who may selectively restrict access.

* Ethereum protocols are sometimes referred to as peer-to-contract systems as well. These are similar to a peer-to-peer systems, but with immutable, persistent programs known as smart contracts taking the place of a peer.

How does DeFiners protocol compare to typical market?

To compete against the typical Decentralized Exchange markets, DeFiners has a unique prediction machine learning algorithm feature that provides simple and straightforward way to confirm trends of the pools. This helps to predict better returns for liquidity providers, and to provide better profits for swappers.
